Our Story

Founded in 2008, Super Pest Control Mumbai started with a simple mission: to provide Mumbai families and businesses with safe, effective, and reliable pest control solutions.

What began as a small local operation has grown into Mumbai's most trusted pest control service. We've built our reputation by combining traditional expertise with modern, eco-friendly methods that prioritize the safety of your family, pets, and the environment.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers across Mumbai, from residential homes to large commercial establishments. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to choice for pest control in the city.
